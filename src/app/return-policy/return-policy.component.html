RETURNS AND REFUND

All our products are eligible for return and refund only if they are stored at a temperature of 0-8 degrees at all time, failing to do so will lead to spoilt juices and we will not be able to provide a refund or take any returns.

All refunds and returns are on the sole discretion of Pressato Food Care Private Limited upon verification by its employees and approval from management. You are allowed to have 2 calendar days to return an item from the date you received it. We insist on the item to be unused and in the same condition as received with its original packaging. Please provide the receipt or proof of purchase for processing the refund.

On receipt of the returned item/items, we will immediately notify you of the status of your refund after inspecting the item. If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your credit card (or original method of payment). You will receive the credit, depending on your card issuer’s policies.

Refunds and returns are deemed valid only if there’s a problem with the product. The refund will be done within 15 days
