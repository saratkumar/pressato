private policy

We are committed in strong sense to protect the privacy of personal information and to handling the personal information in a responsible manner in accordance with the relevant privacy legislation in the countries of our operations (referred to as privacy legislation). In case of us changing the privacy policy (how we handle your personal information), the same will be available on our website. We will collect and store the information furnished to us which will include your name, date of birth, gender and address. The same might be stored either electronically or in print or both. We advise you to inform us if in case you do not wish us to store your data. In that case we might not be able to provide you with the service you expect as we are a subscription based business model. We will treat your personal information as strictly private and confidential. We will use your data for purpose directly related to our core business. We do not sell data for any monetary gains to third parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential and the data is used only for the activities we have asked them to perform. We may release your information when we believe such release is needed to comply with the law, or protect ours or third party rights, property, or safety. Statistical data may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, research or other uses. We have adequate technical and organizational security measures to protect personal data from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
